The story behind the name

Meet the original Genny.

genny is named for my mom — a 90-year-young Frenchwoman born Genevieve. When she came to America, she didn't love how her name sounded in English, so she renamed herself. So her.

Her drink of choice was White Zinfandel — until someone shamed her for it and quietly took the joy right out of her glass. I never forgot that.

genny is for her, and for everyone who just wants something delicious in their hand. No judging. No apology. Just something yummy.

Because what you like matters. Full stop.
